So Who Am I?

My name is Kaiser Paul Nonog.

A student from University of the Cordilleras. Soon to be graduating Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and passionate in making awesome layouts of pictures and websites!

My passion is playing the guitar, and I've been immersed in this journey since 2018. Music has been a profound part of my life, and with every strum of the strings, I find a unique form of self-expression. I also am into reading manga as it's a vibrant and captivating world that has been an integral part of my life for years. Since the moment I picked up my first manga, I've been enthralled by the enchanting blend of storytelling, artistry, and emotion that these graphic novels offer.

I've been also editing and layouting at Adobe Photoshop since 2018 and have been tasked for a commission various of times. Photo editing played a big part of my high school as it has helped me countless of times, which weirdly enough, just started for fun and ultimately resulting into passion.

Skills and Expertise

Frontend: HTML, CSS and JS

Backend: Python and Java Programming

Designing Software: Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator and Figma
